Culinary Masterpieces

Honoring Local Terroir & Avant-Garde Method

At Sterling, dining is not simply a meal — it is a choreographed performance. Directed by our Michelin-starred Executive Chef, our kitchens honor native coastal ingredients and elevate them using refined classic methods.

Whether you dine under the grand chandeliers of L'Ambroisie, enjoy sunset cocktails at the Sky Lounge, or enjoy a custom beach grill layout, each venue offers a distinct sensory memory.
