Smart Stays in the Heart of Cochin

Welcome to CJ Grande by Sterling Hotels & Resorts, a contemporary business hotel conveniently located in Cochin's prime business and shopping hub. Managed by Sterling Hotels & Resorts, CJ Grande is thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of today's business and corporate travelers, while offering comfort and convenience for leisure guests as well.

Our well-appointed Deluxe Rooms and spacious Suites are tailored to suit every budget without compromising on quality or service. Whether you're here for a quick business trip or an extended stay, CJ Grande offers the perfect balance of affordability, accessibility, and comfort.

Located close to key commercial centers, shopping districts, and major tourist attractions, CJ Grande by Sterling Hotels & Resorts allows you to make the most of your time in Cochin – combining productivity with a touch of local exploration.

Why Choose CJ Grande by Sterling Hotels & Resorts?

  • Prime location in Cochin's business and shopping district
  • Comfortable Deluxe Rooms and elegant Suites to suit every traveler
  • Easy access to city landmarks and tourist attractions
  • Seamless connectivity and transport options across the city
  • Warm, professional hospitality by Sterling Hotels & Resorts
