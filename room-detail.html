Deluxe Garden Room

Accommodations Description

Refined Privacy & Comfort

Our Deluxe Garden Rooms represent the ultimate combination of modern architectural layout and warm botanical visuals. Opening out onto private terraces facing the historic property boundaries, each space offers a private respite from the outside world.

Fitted with signature Italian linens, customized soundproofing, touch-based ambient control buttons, and masterfully crafted furniture by local woodworkers. The en-suite bathroom includes heated stone flooring, a deep copper soaking tub, and custom essential oils curated for the hotel.
