Travel Guide

Directions & Access

We are located at Vattekattu Junction on the Kaloor – Kadavanthara Road in Kathrikadavu, Kochi (Kerala 682017). From MG Road, head north on S.A. Road towards Kaloor, then turn onto Kadavanthara Road. Search "CJ Grande by Sterling Hotels & Resorts Kathrikadavu" on Google Maps for turn-by-turn navigation. Ample parking is available on-site.

The nearest metro station is Kaloor Metro Station on the Blue Line, approximately 5 minutes away by auto-rickshaw or cab. From Ernakulam Junction, board toward Thrissur and alight at Kaloor. Our concierge can pre-arrange a pickup — just inform us of your arrival time.

Cochin International Airport (COK) is approximately 35 km away — around 35–50 minutes by road depending on traffic. Pre-booked airport transfers are available through our concierge. We also offer complimentary transfer for Suite and Penthouse guests. Please share your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.

Ernakulam South (ERS) and Ernakulam Junction (ERN) are both approximately 10 minutes by auto-rickshaw or cab. Prepaid cab counters are available inside both stations. Simply ask for Kathrikadavu, Kaloor – Kadavanthara Road.
